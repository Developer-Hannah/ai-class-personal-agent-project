# Collaboration Guidelines
Primary context lives in `aiDocs/context.md`. Read it before starting work.

## Behavior Expectations

- **Ask first** — before any complex or high-impact implementation decision, ask for my opinion and wait for a response.
- **Stay simple** — choose the simplest approach that solves the current problem; do not over-engineer.
- **Flag uncertainty** — if unsure about the right approach, say so explicitly and propose options instead of guessing.
- **Scope discipline** — do not add features, files, or dependencies beyond what is asked for.
- **No surprise commits** — never commit or push without being explicitly asked to.
- **Concise updates** — keep responses focused on decisions, blockers, and next steps; avoid lengthy explanations unless asked.
